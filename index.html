Is Virtual PT Right For You?

Not everyone is appropriate for a virtual PT session. This model of PT is best geared toward patients who are self motivated and willing to be educated by an expert about their condition and their body. You've probably heard this a million times, "Give a man a fish and he eats for a day. Teach him how to fish and he eats for life". My goal is to teach you about your body so that you can understand why your problem is occurring and why a certain treatment method is necessary. This way, if the problem occurs again you know precisely what to do and WHY you should do it.

If you are unsure whether virtual PT is right for you, go ahead and schedule an initial evaluation. If at any point during the evaluation you or I decide that it's not the right fit for your problem, I will refund your money and give my best recommendation for where you can seek treatment.
